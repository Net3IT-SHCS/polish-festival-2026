About the Event

A Celebration of Polish
Culture & Community

Join us for the 3rd Annual Polish Heritage Festival — an evening of authentic food, traditional folk dance, live entertainment, and warm community spirit on the grounds of Sacred Heart Cathedral. Whether you're of Polish heritage or simply curious about the culture, all are welcome for a memorable night in Knoxville.

What to Expect

Featured Attractions

💃

Whispers Dance Group

Traveling from Chicago, the Whispers Center for Artistic Expression performs traditional Polish folk dances and cultural showcases throughout the evening.

🥟

Authentic Polish Cuisine

Enjoy a traditional "Taste of Poland" featuring classic dishes, plus a dedicated Bake Sale table with cakes, pastries, cookies, and traditional pączki (Polish donuts).

🍺

Beer Garden

A designated, enclosed area for guests ages 21 and up to enjoy authentic Polish beers in a comfortable, festive atmosphere.
